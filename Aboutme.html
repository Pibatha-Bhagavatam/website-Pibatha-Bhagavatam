About Me

Namaskaram! I am Daasanudasi, a passionate Carnatic musician and dedicated teacher, carrying forward the rich legacy of my Gurunathai, Smt. Seetha Lakshmi. As the successor and granddaughter of the great Sri Lakshmanan Bhagavatar of Alampallam, Kollengode, I strive to spread the divine essence of sangeetham and Bhagavata dharmam through my teachings. With over 16 years of rigorous training in Carnatic music, I founded Pibatha Sangeetam, where students embark on a structured and devotional journey in music. Our courses cover beginner to advanced levels, offering in-depth training in Swara Sadhana, Raga elaborations, and compositions of great saints like Thyagaraja, Jayadeva, and more. Each class is designed to nurture devotion and discipline, enriching students with the spiritual essence of Bhagavata Dharma. Through my music, I seek to inspire, guide, and uplift students, helping them experience the divine through the beauty of Carnatic melodies.

More About Pibatha Bhagavatam

Pibatha Bhagavatam is a humble initiative dedicated to spreading the profound wisdom of Sanathana Dharma through sacred texts, music, and devotion. Rooted in the teachings of Bhagavata Dharma, this platform serves as a space for spiritual seekers to immerse themselves in the timeless glories of the Bhagavatam, Thiruppavai, Ashtapathy, and the compositions of great saints. Our mission is to preserve and propagate divine knowledge, making it accessible to all who seek spiritual growth and inner transformation. Through structured courses, publications, and devotional discourses, Pibatha Bhagavatam aims to nurture a deep connection with the Bhakti tradition, fostering devotion, discipline, and divine realization.
